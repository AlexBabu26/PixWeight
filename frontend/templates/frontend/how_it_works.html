Understanding Accuracy

Confidence Levels: Each estimate includes a confidence percentage and weight range. Higher confidence means more reliable estimates.

What Improves Accuracy:

  • Clear, well-lit images
  • Reference objects in frame (coins, rulers, etc.)
  • Multiple angles or context clues
  • Accurate answers to AI-generated questions

Limitations:

  • AI estimates are approximate and educational
  • Not suitable for legal, medical, or certified purposes
  • Accuracy varies based on object type and image quality
  • Always verify with actual measurements for critical applications

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is PixWeight?

Accuracy varies by object type and image quality. Most estimates are within 10-30% of actual weight. Complex or unusual objects may have lower accuracy.

Can I use this for shipping?

While we provide shipping cost estimates, always verify weight with a scale for actual shipping to avoid unexpected charges.

What image formats are supported?

JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats up to 10MB. For best results, use high-resolution images with good lighting.

Is my data private?

All images and estimates are private to your account. We use your data to improve the service but never share personal information.
